Our Story

Founded by educators who fell in love with the game

The Digital Chessboard Academy began with a simple belief: that chess is one of the finest teachers a child — or adult — can have. It rewards patience, sharpens focus, and builds the quiet confidence that comes from thinking clearly under pressure.

What started as a handful of online lessons has grown into a global community of learners and coaches. Through every class, we hold to the same standard we started with: teach with care, structure the journey, and celebrate every breakthrough.

Today we coach students across 25+ countries, from their very first move to the tournament hall — and we're only getting started.
