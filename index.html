Questions, Answered

Frequently asked questions

Everything parents usually ask before booking a free demo.

What age can my child start learning chess?
Children can begin from age 3. We adapt the pace and teaching style to each child — and adults are very welcome too. It's never too early or too late to start.
Are classes group or one-on-one (private)?
Both. You can choose small group classes (maximum 7 students) for an interactive, social experience, or fully personalised private 1-on-1 coaching with flexible timing.
Are your coaches qualified?
Yes. Every coach is certified, FIDE-rated and experienced, and trained to teach children and adults with patience and structure.
How do the online classes work?
Classes are live and interactive on a shared digital chessboard — typically 2 classes per week plus weekly tournament practice, with homework, puzzles, and progress tracking between sessions.
Do you offer a free demo class?
Yes — a free, no-obligation demo class. You can book instantly through the calendar, message us on WhatsApp, or leave your details and we'll reach out.
How much do classes cost?
Group plans start at ₹1,999/month, and choosing the quarterly plan saves 20%. Private 1-on-1 coaching is available on enquiry. Every plan includes a free demo and joining gifts.
Will my child receive certificates?
Yes — students receive level completion certificates as they progress, plus exclusive joining gifts including a premium chess training book and a puzzle book.
