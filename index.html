About The Digital Chessboard


Welcome to a world where chess meets fun! We’re a live online chess academy that turns the classic game of strategy into an engaging, interactive learning experience. From beginners to champions, our online chess classes are designed for players of all ages and skill levels across the globe.

We blend traditional chess techniques with modern, gamified coaching, helping you learn, play, and improve at your own pace. Because here, chess isn’t just a game, it’s a fun, lively and an adventure in strategy.
